Overview

Audience

This website will be ideal for civil engineers, students, the general public (10 - 100 years old) interested in obtaining real information about the local climate and other cities for personal or investigative purposes. It is also ideal for people who are dedicated to organizing outdoor events, since they can plan alternatives according to the change of climate to provide a better service. Our audience will be able to visit our website on a recurring basis because we will offer guaranteed and updated information. Also, our website will be structured in a simple way to allow our audience to navigate easily and safely.
